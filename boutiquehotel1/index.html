A hotel receptionist, also known as a front desk receptionist, is a customer service professional who ensures guests have a pleasant stay at a hotel. Their responsibilities include: Greeting guests: Welcoming guests and checking their details against their bookings Assigning rooms: Allocating rooms and suites to guests and providing keys Managing reservations: Handling reservations and booking information by phone, online, or in person Answering questions: Answering questions from guests and prospective customers Processing payments: Processing credit card transactions for lodging, room service, and other hotel costs Checking guests out: Checking guests out, taking payments, and returning deposits Providing information: Providing information about hotel facilities and services, local amenities, and attractions Handling requests: Handling special requests from guests, like booking theater tickets or storing valuable items Dealing with complaints: Dealing with complaints or problems Maintaining records: Maintaining accurate records of guest charges and updating hotel records
